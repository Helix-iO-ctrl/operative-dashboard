What You've Built

Velo is a structured consulting operating system — a complete framework for running AI-powered engagements with small businesses. It's not a set of documents; it's a repeatable delivery machine that lets one person operate with the infrastructure of a small agency.

1 Instant Engagement Setup

The onboarding skill walks through a full client discovery in one session. Within an hour, the system is populated with the client's goals, constraints, assumptions, and opening tasks — ready to run.

2 Persistent Project Memory

Every decision, task, assumption, and session is documented and structured. When a new session starts, Claude reads the context and picks up exactly where you left off — no re-explaining, no dropped threads. This is the capability most small business advisors don't have.

3 Full-Stack Business Coverage

Strategy, operations, marketing, creative, and data — with specific skills for each. Move from "help me define my ideal customer" to "write homepage copy" to "map the client onboarding process" without switching tools or losing context.

4 Client-Facing Dashboard (Live URL)

Every client gets a real-time progress dashboard. It shows task status, phase progress, decisions made, open questions, and a full asset library with in-page previews. This turns you from "consultant who sends emails" into a partner with a live control room.

5 Weekly Snapshot Reports

The weekly-snapshot skill generates a polished, branded one-pager automatically. Shows what got done, velocity trends, blockers with proposed solutions, decisions needed, and next week's plan. Scannable in under 2 minutes by a busy business owner.

6 Transcript Processing

After any client call, drop in a transcript and the system extracts decisions, updates all docs, creates tasks, and refreshes the dashboard. What used to take 2 hours of post-call notes takes 5 minutes.

7 Automated Prospect Onboarding New

When a prospect books via the intake form, the system automatically creates a Supabase project, generates a personalized dashboard with tailored discovery questions, and scaffolds the local project folder. Zero-touch from booking to ready-to-work.

8 Scalable Multi-Client Architecture

Each client is a separate folder with its own system instance. Currently running 4 active engagements in parallel — each fully documented, each with their own live dashboard. Overhead per client is dramatically lower than traditional consulting.

9 Dashboard as Sales Tool New

The auto-generated prospect dashboard doubles as the most powerful sales demo. On the discovery call, the prospect sees their business already mapped out — personalized questions, tailored tasks, their company name in the header. The "holy shit" reaction is built into the product itself.

The Marketing Funnel

Stage 1

Awareness — "Who is this person?"

LinkedIn is the highest-ROI channel. Post weekly about problems solved, client insights, and behind-the-scenes of the system. Referrals from existing client networks (Jason/Helix circles) are the first-mover channel. Cold DMs to founders in the 1–10 employee range who are visibly struggling.

  • "Here's what I did to help [type of business] grow from X to Y in 90 days"
  • "The thing most small businesses are missing that I fix in session one"
  • Before/after screenshots of a live client dashboard
Stage 2

Consideration — "Could this work for me?"

Offer a paid discovery session ($500, credited toward engagement). The hook: the dashboard auto-generates from the booking form, so by the time the call happens, the prospect sees their business already mapped out. The dashboard IS the demo — showing it live on the discovery call produces the strongest conversion moment.

  • Dashboard tease on booking confirmation builds anticipation
  • Personalized discovery questions auto-generated from their intake
  • Case studies and before/after stories (building now)
Stage 3

Conversion — "I want to work with you"

Close with clarity, not pressure. "Here's the 90-day engagement. We start with a discovery call. I set up your dashboard within 48 hours. Every week you get a one-page status report. At the end of 90 days, you have a documented system, a functioning dashboard, and a clear playbook for the next phase."

  • The live dashboard demo does most of the selling
  • $500 discovery session reduces commitment friction
  • Clear onboarding process visible before they sign
Stage 4

Retention — "This is worth every dollar"

Weekly snapshots, live dashboard, documented decision log, and visible progress every single week. The system solves the two biggest causes of churn: feeling like things are slow, or not being sure what you're doing.

  • 90-day sprint → monthly retainer
  • Monthly retainer → refer another business owner → referral credit
  • Every Tier 2 build naturally upsells into Tier 1 ongoing support
