Client Kickoff Process

The standardized workflow for taking a new client from "interested" to actively delivering results in under one week.
0
Pre-Kickoff
15–30 minutes · Before the discovery meeting
📁
Step 1
Copy the client folder template
Duplicate the template folder and rename it for the new client. All skills, task files, and doc structures come pre-loaded.
Hayden
✏️
Step 2
Fill in CLAUDE.md basics
Client name, business type, team size, stage, and a one-sentence description of what they need help with.
Hayden
📅
Step 3
Schedule discovery meeting
45–60 min, video or in-person. Send the pre-call prep message so they come ready.
Hayden
💰
Step 4
Determine engagement type
Free founding (case study), Tier 1 advisory ($2k/mo), or Tier 2 embedded build ($3k–$15k project).
Hayden
1
Discovery Meeting
45–60 minutes · Session 1 with the client
🏢
Block 1
Business fundamentals
What they do, ideal customer, revenue model, revenue range. Get the baseline picture in 10 min.
Hayden + Client
🎯
Block 2
Goals & vision
What does success look like in 90 days? In 12 months? Define the target before building the plan.
Hayden + Client
🚧
Block 3
Constraints & pain points
Biggest blocker, what they've tried, what not to change. Understand the real problem.
Hayden + Client
🤝
Block 4
Resources & the pitch
Budget, team, time commitment. Then show the dashboard demo and explain how Velo works.
Hayden + Client
2
Project Setup
1–2 hours · Within 24 hours of discovery
🤖
Step 1
Cowork processes discovery
Paste notes into Cowork. It populates the business brief, assumptions, tasks across all disciplines, and dashboard data.
Cowork (AI)
📊
Step 2
Dashboard goes live
Create GitHub repo, push dashboard, get a live URL. Client can see real-time progress from day one.
Cowork (AI)
👋
Step 3
Send welcome message
Dashboard link, what to expect, weekly snapshot rhythm, and how decisions get flagged. Sets the tone.
Hayden
📝
Step 4
Case study agreement
For founding clients: send the agreement covering metrics sharing, testimonial, and referral terms.
Hayden
3
First Delivery
2–4 hours across sessions · Within first week
⚡
Step 1
Ship a quick win
Before Week 1 ends, deliver something tangible — a prototype, report, process map, or tool. Builds trust and momentum.
Hayden + Cowork
📋
Step 2
First weekly snapshot
Even after a few days, send the first one-page update. Establishes the weekly rhythm from the start.
Cowork (AI)
🔄
Step 3
Schedule weekly check-in
30 min, same time each week. Client reviews progress, gives feedback, and surfaces new priorities.
Hayden
After Week 1, the client has:
✓ A live dashboard with real-time progress
✓ A fully populated project folder with all docs
✓ At least one tangible deliverable shipped
✓ A weekly snapshot rhythm established
✓ A recurring 30-min check-in on the calendar
✓ Clear expectations for how the engagement works
Repeat for every new client. Copy the template folder, run the process, ship in a week.
