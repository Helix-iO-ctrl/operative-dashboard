Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of businesses do you work with? +
Service businesses and operators with 1–15 employees generating $100k–$2M annually. This includes independent consultants, small agencies (marketing, design, legal, accounting), local service businesses trying to systemize, early-stage startups, and freelancers who are scaling past themselves. If you're growing but feel like the wheels are spinning, this is designed for you.
How is this different from a business coach? +
Business coaching is primarily accountability — check-ins, motivation, habit building. Operative builds systems. At the end of an engagement, you have a documented playbook, a functioning dashboard, and processes that actually exist on paper (and in practice). We execute alongside you, not just talk about it.
What does a typical month look like? +
Usually 2–3 working sessions per month (60–90 minutes each), a Friday snapshot report every week, and async support in between for questions, transcript drops, and document reviews. Sessions can be focused on any business function — strategy one week, marketing the next — depending on your priorities.
What does the live dashboard actually show? +
Your dashboard is a real webpage at a private URL. It shows your current phase of the engagement, tasks by status (done / in progress / upcoming), key decisions made (with reasoning), open questions, velocity tracking, and a progress timeline. It's updated automatically after every session — you don't have to ask what's happening.
Can I cancel if it's not working? +
Yes. Monthly retainers have a 30-day cancellation notice. 90-day engagements are fixed but come with a clear deliverables guarantee — if we're not hitting our goals by the halfway point, we'll discuss an extension or adjustment. We'd rather solve the problem than end the relationship.
How do I know if I'm ready for this? +
You're ready if you have a business generating revenue (even modest), you feel like things are chaotic or stagnant, and you're willing to invest time in sessions and follow through on priorities. You don't need to have it together — that's what the engagement is for. The free discovery call will tell you quickly whether this is the right fit.
