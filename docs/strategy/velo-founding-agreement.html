AI-Powered Business Operations — Pilot Engagement
Section 1

Purpose & Founding Client Program

Velo is an AI-powered consulting operating system that helps small businesses streamline operations, automate workflows, and make data-driven decisions. As a Founding Client, you are among the first businesses to work with Velo. This agreement outlines the terms of our engagement during the founding period.

The Founding Client Program is designed to validate Velo's approach, refine our delivery process, and build real case studies. In exchange for being early adopters, founding clients receive the initial system build at no cost.

Section 2

Scope of Services

During the founding engagement, Velo will provide the following services at no charge:

  • Discovery & Strategy Session — An initial call to understand your business, pain points, goals, and current tools.
  • Custom AI System Build — Design and implementation of AI-powered automations, dashboards, workflows, or tools tailored to your business needs.
  • Project Dashboard — A live, interactive dashboard to track project status, deliverables, and communication.
  • One Round of Revisions — Post-delivery refinements based on your feedback to ensure the system meets your needs.
  • Knowledge Transfer — A walkthrough of the delivered system so you and your team can use it independently.
Section 3

Engagement Duration

The founding engagement begins on the Effective Date and continues until the initial system build and one round of revisions are complete. The expected timeline is 2–4 weeks depending on project complexity, though both parties may agree to extensions.

If, after the initial build is complete, the client wishes to continue building additional systems, features, or automations, both parties will discuss scope and pricing before any paid work begins. There is no obligation to continue beyond the founding engagement.

Section 5

Transition to Paid Engagement

If both parties agree to continue working together beyond the founding build, a separate Statement of Work (SOW) or retainer agreement will be drafted outlining scope, timeline, and pricing. Potential structures include:

  • Monthly Retainer — Ongoing support, maintenance, and iterative improvements at a fixed monthly rate.
  • Project-Based — Scoped engagements for specific new builds or expansions.
  • Hourly Advisory — Ad-hoc consulting on strategy, AI implementation, or operations.

No paid work will begin without the client's explicit written agreement.

Section 6

Ownership & Intellectual Property

All custom-built systems, automations, dashboards, and deliverables created specifically for the client during this engagement are owned by the client upon delivery. Velo retains the right to use anonymized versions of the work for portfolio, case study, and marketing purposes unless the client opts out in writing.

Velo retains ownership of any proprietary frameworks, templates, or tools that existed prior to or were developed independently of this engagement.

Section 7

Case Study & Testimonial

As a founding client, you agree to allow Velo to create a case study based on your engagement. This may include a brief description of the problem solved, the approach taken, and the results achieved. Your business name and details will be included unless you request anonymization. If applicable, Velo may request a short testimonial or quote for use on the Velo website and marketing materials.

Section 8

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary business information, trade secrets, customer data, or sensitive operational details shared during this engagement. This obligation survives the end of the engagement.

Section 9

Termination

Either party may end this engagement at any time with written notice (email is sufficient). If the client terminates before the initial build is complete, Velo will deliver any work completed to date. There are no penalties or fees for early termination.

Section 10

Limitation of Liability

Velo provides services on an "as-is" basis during the founding period. While Velo will use best efforts to deliver high-quality systems, Velo is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of delivered systems. The client is responsible for reviewing and testing all deliverables before deploying them in production environments.
