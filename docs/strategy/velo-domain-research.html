Domain Research Results

Research conducted March 11, 2026. Original target velo.io was ruled out due to premium pricing. Five affordable alternatives identified and ranked.

Awaiting Client Selection
Ruled Out
velo.io
Premium pricing — $5,000+ from aftermarket sellers. Not viable for a bootstrapped launch.
Available Alternatives
Recommended
getvelo.io
~$30–60/yr
Top pick — action-oriented, clean. The "get" prefix is widely used by successful SaaS brands (getform.io, getharvest.com). Immediately communicates action and acquisition.
Strong Alt
usevelo.io
~$30–60/yr
Strong alternative — implies utility and hands-on engagement. "Use" positions Velo as a tool clients actively work with, not just a service they hire.
runvelo.io
~$30–60/yr
Good — implies operational speed and execution. Aligns well with the "velocity" brand meaning. Great if you want to emphasize the AI-operations angle.
hirevelo.io
~$30–60/yr
Good for advisory positioning — frames Velo as a partner you bring on. Works well for the consulting angle if you lean into the advisory retainer model.
usevelo.com
~$10–15/yr
Available .com fallback — lower cost, broader recognition. The .com TLD carries more trust for some audiences, though .io signals tech-forward positioning.
Team Recommendation
We recommend getvelo.io or usevelo.io as the primary domain. Both are affordable (~$30–60/yr), available at standard registration pricing, and align with the Velo brand. Marketing team input has been requested before final selection. Once Hayden picks a domain, the team will register it and update all references across landing pages, dashboards, and marketing materials.
