Why These Colors

Amber as the primary color is a deliberate break from every other AI consulting brand using navy, electric blue, or purple. Amber signals energy, momentum, and warmth — qualities that matter when you're asking a small business owner to trust you with their company.

The warm off-white background (#FBF7F2) makes every surface feel inviting rather than clinical. Combined with Deep Night for type, it reads as premium without feeling cold or corporate.

No pure black, no pure white. Both are softened intentionally. This keeps the palette feeling human and approachable — which is the core brief.

The palette as a whole sits closer to a high-end independent brand (think boutique agency, premium SaaS) than a generic tech startup. That distinction matters for Velo's positioning: you're not selling software, you're selling judgment.
