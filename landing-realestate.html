The Wholesale Bottleneck

📞

Lead follow-up is inconsistent

You pull a list, skip trace it, blast it out — and then half the callbacks go unanswered because you're on another call or driving to a property.

📊

Your pipeline is in your head

Which sellers need a second offer? Who said "call me next month"? What's under contract? You're guessing instead of tracking.

🏠

Comps and ARV take forever

Every deal means pulling comps, estimating rehab, running numbers. You're doing the same research process over and over — manually.

📝

Disposition is a scramble

You get a property under contract and then scramble to blast your buyer's list, answer questions, and coordinate showings — all while working the next deal.

💸

Marketing spend with no tracking

Direct mail, PPC, driving for dollars, cold calling — you're spending money across channels but can't tell which ones are actually producing deals.

🔄

Can't scale without hiring

You're doing 2-3 deals a month solo and maxed out. The only way to do more is to hire — but you're not sure the margins support it yet.

Works for Every Wholesale Model

Whether you're a solo wholesaler or building a team, Velo adapts to your operation.

You're doing everything — marketing, acquisition, dispo, closing. AI handles the repetitive work so you can focus on the high-dollar activities: talking to sellers and making offers.
