Lead-to-Discovery Automation

When a lead fills out the landing page form, this flow runs automatically — creating their project folder, pre-filling CLAUDE.md, booking the call, and generating a prep brief so Hayden walks in ready.
📝
Form Submit
The Lead
🌐
Trigger

Lead fills out landing page form

Intake form on the Velo small business landing page captures business details, pain points, and goals. Takes the lead 3–5 minutes.

📋
Captured

Form fields collected

Name, email, business name, business type, team size, revenue range, biggest challenge, what success looks like, and time availability.

📅
Redirect

Scheduling page opens

After submission, the lead lands on a Cal.com/Calendly booking page to pick a 45-min discovery call slot. Confirmation email sent automatically.

⚡ Automated
⚡
Auto-Setup
Automation
📁
Step 1

Client folder created from template

Webhook triggers a script that copies the client-folder-template, renames it to the business name, and places it in the working directory.

⚡ Automated
✏️
Step 2

CLAUDE.md pre-filled with form data

Business name, contact, email, type, team size, stage, and the lead's own description of what they want help with — all injected into the template.

⚡ Automated
🔔
Step 3

Hayden gets notified

Email/SMS notification with the lead's details, their booked time slot, and a link to the project folder. No action needed yet.

⚡ Automated
🤖
AI Prep Brief
Cowork (AI)
🔍
Step 1

Analyze the lead's responses

Cowork reads the pre-filled CLAUDE.md, researches the business online (website, LinkedIn, reviews), and identifies the likely pain points and opportunities.

★ AI-Powered
💡
Step 2

Propose engagement type

Based on business size, challenge, and budget signals — recommends Free Founding, Tier 1 Advisory ($2k/mo), or Tier 2 Embedded Build ($3k–$15k). Includes reasoning.

★ AI-Powered
📊
Step 3

Generate discovery prep brief

One-page document with: business summary, recommended engagement, key questions to ask, potential quick wins to mention, and talking points for the pitch.

★ AI-Powered
👤

Discovery Call
Hayden + Lead
