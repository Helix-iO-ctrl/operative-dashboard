Executive Summary

Operative is entering a market where demand is outpacing supply and most competitors are either too expensive or too generic. The go-to-market strategy is three phases: land 2–3 founding clients for free or heavily discounted to build case studies and social proof, then use those assets to drive paid acquisition on LinkedIn and through referrals. The total investment to get to $10k MRR is achievable with as little as $200/month in hard costs. At the $500–$800/month tier, you can meaningfully accelerate reach.

Section 02

Target Audiences

Two distinct audiences, two distinct messages. Both benefit from the same product, but they need to hear completely different things to take action.

Audience A — Consultant Track

The Aspiring AI Consultant

  • Age 22–32, early career or career-switching
  • Digitally native, LinkedIn-active, entrepreneurially minded
  • Has some business or tech background — not a developer
  • Currently freelancing, in corporate, or between jobs
  • Motivated by: income freedom, being ahead of the curve, credential-building
  • Pain: Doesn't know where to start. Has no system, no credibility, no clients yet.
  • Buying stage: Consideration → Decision
  • Where they hang out: LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Reddit (r/freelance, r/consulting), YouTube
Audience B — Small Business Track

The Growing Small Business Owner

  • Age 30–50, owner or founder of a 1–15 person service business
  • Generating $100k–$2M annually, growing but chaotic
  • Has tried coaches, maybe a consultant — was underwhelmed
  • Motivated by: visibility, systems, scaling without burning out
  • Pain: No time, no infrastructure, feels like spinning wheels. Every call re-explains everything.
  • Buying stage: Awareness → Consideration
  • Where they hang out: LinkedIn, local business communities, referral networks, podcasts

🎯 Priority Target for Phase 1 (Founding Clients)

Start with Audience B: Small Business Owners in Hayden's existing network — coaches, operators, and founders in the Helix / Jason / Jake / Keagan circles. These are warm relationships where trust already exists. Offer the first 2–3 engagements at $0 or $500 (heavily reduced) in exchange for a detailed case study, dashboard screenshots, and a recorded testimonial.

Section 05

Founding Client Strategy
"The Case Study First" Model

Before you spend a dollar on ads, you need proof. No case study = no credibility = ads don't convert. Here's how to get the first 2–3 case studies fast.

✅ The Founding Client Offer

Option A (Free): Full 90-day engagement at no cost. In exchange: a detailed case study, 2 testimonial quotes, permission to use dashboard screenshots, and a 15-minute recorded video testimonial.

Option B ($500 Discovery + $1,000/mo): Paid discovery session + reduced retainer. In exchange: same case study terms. This validates that the client has skin in the game while staying affordable.

Option C (Partial Barter): Exchange consulting hours for something the client offers (e.g., a fitness coach gives Hayden 3 months of coaching in exchange for 90 days of advisory). Reduces cash friction entirely.

Where to Find Founding Clients (No Ads Needed)

  1. Helix network (Jason, Jake, Keagan's circles) — already mentioned in the capabilities doc. This is the first outreach target. Send a personal message, not a template.
  2. Former colleagues and managers — anyone who has run a small business or team and complained about operations, systems, or growth.
  3. LinkedIn 1st-degree connections — filter for founders, owners, operators at small businesses. DM 5–10 personally crafted messages per week.
  4. Facebook/LinkedIn local business groups — post "I'm offering a free advisory engagement in exchange for a case study" with the specific outcomes and deliverables listed.
  5. Reddit r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur — share the value first (insight posts, answering questions), then mention the founding client offer in comments when relevant.

The Outreach Script (DM Template)

Hey [Name] — I know you've been building [Company] for a while now. I'm launching something I think could genuinely help you.

I've built an AI-powered advisory system that gives you a live business dashboard, weekly progress reports, and full strategic coverage — similar to what a boutique consulting firm would deliver, but at a fraction of the cost.

I'm offering the first 2–3 businesses a fully free 90-day engagement in exchange for a case study. No catch — just want to build real proof.

Would you be up for a 30-minute call to see if it makes sense?

Here's a quick look at what the system does: [Loom link]
